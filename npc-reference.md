# NPC Reference — Ghost Rider Stat Blocks

*These are the CR 6 / CR 19 / CR 26 stat blocks for running Ghost Rider as an NPC, antagonist, or encounter. They are NOT the leveling class — the class is in the [guidebook](ghost-rider-player-dm-guidebook.md). These are what the DM uses when Ghost Rider appears in a campaign as a character in the world.*

---

## Inheritance Chain

```
Johnny Blaze (CR 6, Expert 7, Human)
    ↓ transforms into
Ghost Rider (CR 19, 18 HD, Medium Outsider)
    ↓ surges into
Zarathos (CR 26, 26 HD, Large Outsider)
```

Each form retains all abilities of forms below it. Chain weapon feats are NOT inherited by Zarathos (he uses natural attacks).

**Immune:** fire, empyreal (self), fear, death effects, disease, poison, fatigue, exhaustion, bleed.
**Resist:** acid 20, cold 20, electricity 20.
**Weakness:** Heaven-forged weapons bypass DR/suppress regen; loved one's name (DC 22 Will or can't attack that creature 1 min).

### Three Auras (Always Active)

**Existential Dread (Su) — 60 ft., DC 30 Will.** Soul-based, not mind-affecting. Full tier table below.

**Hellfire Aura (Su) — 10 ft.** 4d6 empyreal/rd, DC 30 Ref half. Suppressible (free action).

**Smoldering Presence (Su) — 30 ft.** Plants wilt, water steams, animals flee. No damage, no save.

### Attacks

**Melee:** Hellfire chain +32/+27/+22/+17 (2d6+16, 19–20, plus 3d6 empyreal)
**Power Attack:** +24/+19/+14/+9 (2d6+32 plus 3d6 empyreal)
**Ranged:** Hellfire blast +22 touch (8d6, 60 ft.) OR Shotgun +22 touch (6d6, 40 ft.)
**Chain whip:** +22 touch, 30 ft. (soul binding + 3d6 empyreal/rd)

### NPC Penance Stare (DC 40)

**Contact:** Opposed Intimidate (+42) for eye contact. Voluntary = immediate lock.
**Judgment Lock:** Paralyzed. Only physical intervention or GR's choice ends it.
**Fail:** 4d4 Wis/round. At Wis 0: catatonic, soul consumed.
**Success:** 1 Wis flat/round. Lock holds.

### Other Abilities

**Soul Binding (DC 30 Fort):** Any chain hit. Paralyzed, no magical movement, −8 concentration, 3d6 empyreal/rd.
**Cold Hellfire Touch (3/day):** 4d6 Wis, DC 30 Fort half.
**Hellfire Nova (1/day):** 10d6 empyreal, 30 ft., DC 30 Ref half.
**Brimstone Impact (1/day):** 20d6 empyreal + 10d6 bludgeoning, 30 ft., DC 30/28 Ref half.
**Hellfire Breath (3/day):** 6d8, 30 ft. cone, DC 26 Ref half.

**Feats (25):** Cleave, Combat Reflexes, Cornugon Smash, Diehard, Dodge, Dreadful Carnage, Endurance, Furious Focus, Great Cleave, Great Fortitude, Imperious Command, Improved Bull Rush, Improved Critical (spiked chain), Improved Disarm, Improved Grapple, Indomitable Faith, Intimidating Prowess, Iron Will, Lightning Reflexes, Lunge, Power Attack, Shatter Defenses, Toughness, Weapon Focus (spiked chain), Weapon Specialization (spiked chain).

**Skills:** Intimidate +42, Perception +31, Sense Motive +31, Knowledge (planes/religion) +22, Bluff +32, Ride +29, Stealth +25, Acrobatics +22.

## NPC Existential Dread — Soul Awareness Tiers

DC 30 (Ghost Rider) / DC 35 (Zarathos). Soul-based Will save. Not mind-affecting.

| Tier | Who | On Failed Save | On Successful Save |
|---|---|---|---|
| **0** | No soul (constructs, mindless undead, oozes) | **Immune** (Zarathos: −2 attacks/skills, no save) | — |
| **1** | Animal soul (INT 1–2) | Flees at max speed | −1 all checks |
| **2** | Mortal soul (INT 3+) | Shaken 1d6 rds (evil: Frightened) | No effect |
| **3** | Aware soul (divine casters, 10+ ranks religion/planes) | Frightened 1d4 rds | Shaken 1 round |
| **4** | Cosmic soul (outsiders, intelligent undead, demigods) | Panicked 1d4 rds | Frightened 1 round |
| **5** | Soul-thieves (liches, soul-consuming demons) | Panicked + DC 30/35 or Cowering 1 rd | Frightened 1d4 rds |
| **6** | Divine (gods) | Frightened 1d6 rds | Shaken 1d6 rds |

Death-workers with divine sanction = Tier 2 at worst (professional courtesy).

## Playing Against Ghost Rider

**Party's best tools:** Soulless entities (suppress abilities, −4 within 30 ft.), Heaven-forged weapons (suppress regen), deny soul consumption, the loved one's name (DC 22 Will), Soul Hunger attrition (deny souls over sessions, −2/day to all rolls, max −10).

**Worst instincts:** Out-damage without suppressing regen, close range without addressing Hellfire Aura, fear/mind-affecting effects (immune), splitting the party inside Dread range.
