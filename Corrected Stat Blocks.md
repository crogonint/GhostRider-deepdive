# Ghost Rider — Corrected Stat Blocks (All Three Forms)
### Post-Audit / Inheritance-Chain Applied
*Penance Stare: DC 40 Will, 4d4 Wis/round on fail, 1 flat on success (Package 5 — final)*

---

## Inheritance Chain

```
Johnny Blaze (CR 6, human Expert)
    ↓ transforms into
Ghost Rider (CR 19, Outsider) — inherits all Blaze feats + special qualities
    ↓ surges into
Zarathos (CR 26, Large Outsider) — inherits all GR + Blaze feats + qualities
```

Each form retains all abilities of forms below it in the chain.

4d6 empyreal damage each round to all within range (DC 27 Ref half). Bypasses all fire resistance and immunity without exception. May be suppressed as a free action. Automatically suppressed in Blaze Leading mode. Cannot be suppressed in Zarathos Surging mode.

**2. Aura of Righteous Terror (Su)** — 60 ft. radius

Non-magical plants wilt and grey (die if stationary 1+ minute). Ambient flames dim one step. Standing water steams. Animals flee (DC 30 Handle Animal to keep calm). No damage, no save — atmospheric only.

# ZARATHOS
## The Ancient Fire / Unbound Demon-Angel

**CR 26** · XP 2,457,600
CE Large Outsider (chaotic, demon, evil, extraplanar, empyreal)
**Init** +9 · **Senses** Darkvision 120 ft., true seeing, Scent Sin (unlimited range, same plane); Perception +45

---

### Defense

**AC** 40 (touch 32, FF 35)
> +5 Dex, +8 natural, +12 deflection, +6 profane, −1 size
> *Correction from original: both deflection and profane apply to touch AC in PF1E*

**HP** 497 (25d10+350+3); Regeneration 20 (good weapons or artifacts of Heaven)
> *CON corrected 36→38; Toughness inherited; HP recalculated*

**Fort** +30 · **Ref** +21 · **Will** +24
> Fort: base +14 + CON +14 + Great Fortitude +2
> Ref: base +14 + DEX +5 + Lightning Reflexes +2
> Will: base +14 + WIS +7 + Iron Will +2 + Indomitable Faith +1 *(all three now documented)*

**SR** 37 · **Channel Resistance** +6

**Speed** 50 ft., fly 60 ft. (perfect)

**Melee**

**Ranged**

### Auras (Three Active at All Times)

**1. Hellfire Aura (Su)** — 30 ft. radius
8d6 empyreal damage each round (DC 34 Ref half). Bypasses fire resistance up to 30 points. Fire-immune creatures still take 4d6 empyreal (soul component). Cannot be suppressed.

**2. Aura of Ancient Dread (Su)** — 120 ft. radius
- All creatures: frightened 1d6 rounds (DC 34 Will reduces to shaken 1 round)
- Evil creatures failing by 10+: panicked 2d6 rounds instead
- Successful saves: shaken 1 round regardless

**3. Smoldering Presence (Su)** — 60 ft. radius, passive
All non-magical plant life within 60 ft. dies within 1 round. Water evaporates within 10 ft. Constructed fires and lights within 30 ft. gutter and die unless magically maintained. Air becomes acrid and sulphurous. Animals within 120 ft. flee at maximum speed and do not stop.
