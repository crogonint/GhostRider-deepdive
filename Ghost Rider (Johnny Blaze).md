# Ghost Rider — Johnny Blaze / Zarathos Composite

![](images/Ghost_Rider.png)


> [!abstract] Designer Note — The Empyreal Descriptor
> Hellfire is described in Marvel canon as **"empyreal"** — from the Empyrean, the highest Heaven. It is not holy. It is not unholy. It is **divine judgment fire** that burns the soul. In PF1E terms, the **[empyreal]** descriptor bypasses all fire resistance, all fire immunity, all holy resistance, and all unholy resistance. See [Canon Deep Dive](Canon%20Deep%20Dive.md) for full descriptor rules.

> [!abstract] Inheritance Chain
> Johnny Blaze (CR 6 · Expert 7 · Human) transforms into Ghost Rider (CR 19 · 18 HD · Outsider).
> Ghost Rider surges into Zarathos (CR 26 · 26 HD · Large Outsider).
> Each form retains all abilities of forms below it.

### Johnny's Special Qualities

> Even in human form, Zarathos bleeds through faintly. Evil creatures entering or starting their turn within 10 ft. sense the divine judgment aura and must succeed on a DC 16 Will save or be shaken for 1 round. Soul-based, not mind-affecting. Mindless creatures immune.

## Auras — Three Active at All Times

> [!warning] 1. Aura of Existential Dread (Su) — 60 ft. radius
> **Always active. Cannot be suppressed. Soul-based and supernatural — not mind-affecting.**
> Bypasses immunity to fear and immunity to mind-affecting for any creature possessing a soul (living, undead, outsiders). Mindless creatures (constructs, oozes, plants, vermin with no INT score, mindless undead) are immune.
>
> On entering the aura or at the start of each round within it, affected creatures must make a **DC 30 Will save**:
>
> | Soul Awareness Tier | Who | Fail | Success |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | **Tier 0** | No soul — constructs, mindless undead, oozes | **Immune** | — |
> | **Tier 1** | Animal soul (INT 1–2) | Flees at max speed | −1 all checks, uneasy |
> | **Tier 2** | Mortal soul (sapient humanoids, INT 3+) | Shaken 1d6 rds (evil: Frightened) | No effect |
> | **Tier 3** | Aware soul — divine casters, 10+ ranks Knowledge religion/planes, anyone who has witnessed divine power firsthand | Frightened 1d4 rds | Shaken 1 round — they know what they felt |
> | **Tier 4** | Cosmic soul — all outsiders, intelligent undead who chose undeath (liches, vampires), demigods, Empyreal Lords | Panicked 1d4 rds | Frightened 1 round — no save removes this entirely |
> | **Tier 5** | Soul-thieves — those who imprison souls against natural law, liches with phylacteries, soul-consuming demons | Panicked 1d4 rds + DC 30 or Cowering 1 round | Frightened 1d4 rds — no clean success |
> | **Tier 6** | Divine — gods, greater deities | Frightened 1d6 rds (divine will prevents full panic) | Shaken 1d6 rds — even gods feel this |
>
> **Death-workers with divine sanction** (psychopomps, angels of death performing legitimate soul collection) recognize Ghost Rider as peer authority. They treat the aura as Tier 2 at worst and are not subject to terror — they sense the Dread but are unafraid. They know whose employ he is in.
>
> **Nat-1:** Maximum-duration failure at tier's effect.
> **Nat-20:** Succeeds, but the Dread Condition persists 24 hrs. Soul awareness cannot be unlearned.
>
> **Spellcasting within aura:** DC 26 concentration check to cast any spell with the [emotion] or [fear] descriptor, or any spell requiring concentration. Failure wastes the action and slot.
>
> **Imperious Command interaction:** When Ghost Rider successfully demoralizes a target via Cornugon Smash or active Intimidate check, the target Cowers 1 round before the shaken condition applies — even if already shaken from this aura.

> [!warning] 2. Hellfire Aura (Su) — 10 ft. radius
> 4d6 empyreal damage each round to all within range (DC 30 Ref half). Bypasses all fire resistance, fire immunity, holy/unholy resistance without exception. **May be suppressed** as a free action. Automatically suppressed in Blaze Leading mode. Cannot be suppressed in Zarathos Surging mode.

> [!warning] 3. Smoldering Presence (Su) — 30 ft. radius, passive
> Non-magical plants wilt and grey within 30 ft. (die if Ghost Rider stationary 1+ minute). Ambient flames dim one illumination step. Standing water steams. Animals flee (DC 30 Handle Animal to keep calm). No damage, no save — purely atmospheric. Always the first sign the Rider is coming.

## Special Abilities

> [!note] Penance Stare + Judgment Lock (Su)

![](images/Penance_Stare.jpg)

> **Standard action · Range 30 ft. · Soul-based, not mind-affecting**
>
> **Establishing contact:** Requires line of sight. Against an aware, unwilling target, Ghost Rider must succeed on an opposed Intimidate check (move action, Intimidate +42) to force eye contact. A creature that averts its eyes has a 20% miss chance but is not subject to the stare. A creature that closes its eyes is blinded but safe. Voluntary eye contact is immediately locked — no check.
>
> **Judgment Lock:** Once contact is established, the target is paralyzed. Cannot break the lock through any act of will. No saving throw ends it. Only physical external intervention (a creature stepping between them) or Ghost Rider choosing to release ends the effect.
>
> **DC 40 Will** (rolled once, on initial contact):
> - **Fail:** Paralyzed + **4d4 Wisdom damage per round** the lock is maintained. At Wis 0: catatonic. Ghost Rider may consume the soul entirely. Restoration requires *miracle*, *wish*, or DC 45 Heal (9th-level divine caster only). If soul consumed: no restoration by any mortal means.
> - **Success:** Shaken 1d4 rounds + **1 Wisdom damage** (flat, not a die) per round the lock is maintained. The lock still holds — success means they resist the full weight, not that they escape.
>
> **Immunities:** Soul-based. Bypasses immunity to fear, mind-affecting, and paralysis for any creature with a soul. Mindless creatures (constructs, oozes, plants, vermin with no INT, mindless undead) are immune.
>
> **Benchmark rounds:** Street mortal ~1 · Elite caster ~3 · Empyreal Lord ~15 · Demon Lord ~12 · Lesser Deity ~26 · Greater Deity ~31

> [!note] Hellfire Chain — Divine Weapon (Su)

![](images/Hellfire_Chain.png)

> **Free action to summon/dismiss · Reach 20 ft. (with Lunge) · Proficiency inherent**
>
> Functions as a +4 anarchic flaming burst spiked chain dealing empyreal damage. Responds to mental commands — Ghost Rider can redirect it as a move action without using a hand.
>
> **Soul Binding** (DC 30 Fort — triggers on any successful chain strike; negates binding, not damage):
> - Target is **paralyzed** — soul pinned to the material plane, body cannot act against the chain's will
> - **Cannot teleport, dimension door, plane shift, ethereal jaunt, or use any magical movement** — soul is anchored
> - **−8 penalty** to concentration and caster level checks
> - **3d6 empyreal damage per round** automatically while bound
>
> **Breaking free:** DC 38 Strength check (full-round action) OR *break enchantment* / *freedom* at CL 30+ OR artifact-level magic. Standard magic does not work. This binds the soul.
>
> The chain can also **disarm** (+32) and **sunder** (+32). On a confirmed critical, the chain automatically attempts soul bind even if the Fort save was previously made this encounter.

> [!note] Cold Hellfire Touch (Su)
> **3/day · Standard action · Melee touch**
>
> Deals no fire or physical damage. Inflicts **4d6 Wisdom damage** (DC 30 Fort half). A creature reduced to 0 Wis is soul-shocked — consciousness expelled, drifting helplessly (as *magic jar* without anchor) until Wis is restored. Ghost Rider uses this reluctantly. Zarathos does not.

> [!note] Trail of Hellfire (Su)
> **Activates automatically at Hellcycle speed 60+ ft.**
>
> A wall of empyreal flame: **10 ft. wide × 15 ft. tall**, any length traversed this round. Creatures entering or crossing: **6d6 empyreal damage** (DC 25 Ref half). Persists 1 minute. Ghost Rider extinguishes as a free action. At 15 ft. height, the trail cannot be cleared by non-magical jumps or fly speeds ≤15 ft.

> [!note] Brimstone Impact (Su)
> **1/day · Full-round action · Minimum 30 ft. drop**
>
> Ghost Rider launches from height and impacts like a divine meteor. No other actions taken that turn.
> - **Radius:** 30 ft.
> - **Damage:** 20d6 empyreal + 10d6 bludgeoning (DC 30 Ref half for empyreal; DC 28 Ref half for bludgeoning)
> - **Concussive wave:** DC 30 Fort or knocked prone + pushed 2d6×5 ft. from impact point
> - **Structural:** All structures within 30 ft. take 20d6 damage (hardness applies). Vehicles within 15 ft. overturned automatically. Ground within 30 ft. becomes difficult terrain.
> - Ghost Rider takes no damage from the landing.

> [!note] Hellfire Nova (Su)
> **1/day · Free action (post-transformation) or standard action**
>
> 10d6 empyreal in a 30 ft. burst (DC 30 Ref half). Ghost Rider immune. When Zarathos is Surging: **15d6 empyreal, no save**.

> [!note] Hellfire Breath (Su)
> **3/day · Standard action · 30 ft. cone**
>
> 6d8 empyreal (DC 26 Ref half). May also be used as a targeted spit (+22 ranged touch, single target, 4d8 empyreal). Chain-web variant: DC 26 Ref or entangled in a 15 ft. cone of burning chain links — soul binding applies on failed Ref save.

> [!note] Burning Judgment Eyes (Su)
> **Constant · Passive**
>
> Grants all of the following simultaneously, no action required:
> - *True seeing* (permanent, unlimited CL)
> - **Sin sight:** Ghost Rider knows the general category of sins a creature carries (murder, betrayal, cruelty). Not a save — it is knowledge.
> - *Detect evil* (constant)
> - **Supernatural awareness:** Cannot be surprised by evil creatures. Cannot be flanked by evil-aligned creatures.
> - *Arcane sight* + *detect magic* equivalent (aura sight, constant)

> [!note] Blaze's Will — Unrestricted (Ex)
> - Ghost Rider's alignment remains CN regardless of Zarathos's influence.
> - **1/day:** If compelled to harm an innocent, immediate DC 22 Will save (free action) — success negates the compulsion, dazed 1 round.
> - **No CR cap:** Ghost Rider may resist direct commands from any entity of any CR, including Mephisto. Particularly overwhelming sources may add up to +10 to DC (GM discretion), but it is never automatic.
> - Immune to Zarathos's direct possession unless unconscious or critically fails DC 22 Will at a moment of extreme trauma.

> [!note] Undying (Ex/Su)
> Cannot be permanently slain while Zarathos is bound. Falls at 0 hp; rises in 1d10 rounds at 1 hp. Exceptions: Heaven-forged weapon, 9th-level good divine spell (DC 30 CL check), or divine artifact. On such a death, Zarathos is expelled and Blaze is left as a helpless human.

> [!note] The Unbreakable Bond (Ex)
> The bond between Blaze and Zarathos was sealed by the archangel Zadkiel and ultimately by the authority of the One Above All:
> - **Mephisto cannot alter the terms.** He uses proxies and deception instead.
> - **No cosmic entity can steal or permanently break the bond** without direct divine override.
> - **Zarathos cannot be claimed by another Hell Lord.** The bond is personal and non-transferable without Blaze's explicit consent.
> - This does not protect Johnny from harm or manipulation — it protects the *nature of the bond itself*.

> [!note] Hell-Tempered Leathers (Ex)
> Johnny's jacket, pants, boots, and gloves function as **+2 leather armor** (human form) / **+3 ghost touch leather armor** (Ghost Rider form):
> - Immune to all fire — mundane, magical, empyreal included
> - Cannot be damaged by non-magical weapons (Hardness 15, 30 hp vs. magical damage)
> - Cannot be removed while the bond is active
> - In Ghost Rider form, may manifest spectral shoulder spikes

## Background

![](images/GR_Haunt.jpg)


Johnny Blaze grew up in the Quentin Carnival, second-generation stunt rider. His adoptive father Crash Simpson was dying of cancer. Johnny, already deep in occult study, summoned what he thought was Satan — he got Mephisto, which is strictly worse. The deal cured the cancer. Mephisto had Crash die in a stunt accident anyway. Johnny has never forgiven himself for the wording.

Mephisto bonded Zarathos to Johnny as an act of spite. The bond was later sealed beyond Mephisto's power to break by the archangel Zadkiel — and ultimately by the authority of the One Above All. Johnny has spent decades fighting for the wheel, losing often enough to know the cost, winning often enough to know it's worth it. He holds nine Guinness World Records. Doctor Strange once noted him as a potential Sorcerer Supreme. He has been to Hell three times. He walked out twice on his own. The third time, an angel opened the door.
