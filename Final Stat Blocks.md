### Auras

**1. Aura of Existential Dread (Su)** — 60 ft. radius, always active, cannot be suppressed
*Soul-based supernatural effect. Not mind-affecting. Bypasses immunity to fear and mind-affecting for all creatures with a soul (living, undead, outsiders). Mindless creatures (constructs, oozes, plants, vermin with no INT score, mindless undead) are immune.*

DC **30** Will save on entering aura or at start of each round within it.

| Soul Awareness Tier | Who | Fail | Success |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tier 0** | No soul (constructs, mindless undead, oozes) | **Immune** | — |
| **Tier 1** | Animal soul (INT 1–2) | Flees at max speed | −1 all checks, uneasy |
| **Tier 2** | Mortal soul (sapient humanoids, INT 3+) | Shaken 1d6 rds (evil: Frightened) | No effect |
| **Tier 3** | Aware soul (divine casters, 10+ ranks Knowledge religion/planes, anyone who has witnessed divine power firsthand) | Frightened 1d4 rds | Shaken 1 round — they know what they felt |

| **Tier 6** | Divine (gods, greater deities) | Frightened 1d6 rds (divine will prevents full panic) | Shaken 1d6 rds — even gods feel this |

> **Death-workers with divine sanction** (psychopomps, angels of death, heralds of Pharasma performing legitimate soul collection) recognize Ghost Rider as a peer authority and are not subject to terror from this aura. > **Nat-1:** Maximum duration failure at tier's effect.
> **Nat-20:** Succeeds, but the Dread Condition persists 24 hrs. Soul awareness cannot be unlearned.
> **Spellcasting:** Spellcasters within the aura must succeed on a **DC 26 concentration check** to cast any spell with the [emotion] or [fear] descriptor, or any spell requiring concentration. Failure wastes the action and the spell slot.

### Special Abilities (All Three Forms)

**Sin Sight (Su)**
Ghost Rider (and Zarathos) perceives the moral weight of a creature's actions as a visible aura. Alignment is visible as colour and intensity. More significantly: GR perceives a creature's *guilt* — not its alignment, but the specific acts of harm it has committed. A Lawful Good creature that has committed specific acts of cruelty glows with those acts. A Chaotic Evil creature that has never personally harmed an innocent glows dimly. Sin Sight overrides magical concealment of alignment.

**Penance Stare + Judgment Lock (Su)**
*Standard action · Range 30 ft. · DC 40 Will · 4d4 Wis/round on fail, 1 flat on success (Package 5 — final)*

*Establishing contact:* Requires line of sight. Against an aware, unwilling target, Ghost Rider must succeed on an opposed Intimidate check (move action, Intimidate +42/+60) to force eye contact. A creature that averts its eyes has a 20% miss chance on Ghost Rider (and GR on it) but is not subject to the stare. A creature that closes its eyes is blinded but safe.

*Voluntary contact:* A creature that meets Ghost Rider's gaze willingly is immediately lock-in with no check required.

*Judgment Lock:* Once contact is established, the target is paralyzed. It cannot break contact through any act of will. No saving throw ends the lock — only physical external intervention (a creature stepping between them) or Ghost Rider choosing to release ends it.

*DC 40 Will (rolled on initial contact):*
- **Fail:** Paralyzed + 4d4 Wis damage per round. At Wis 0: catatonic. Ghost Rider may consume their soul. Restoration requires *miracle*, *wish*, or DC 45 Heal (9th-level divine caster) — if anything worth restoring remains.
- **Success:** Shaken 1d4 rounds + 1 Wis damage per round.

*Soul-based, not mind-affecting. Bypasses immunity to fear, mind-affecting, and paralysis for creatures possessing a soul. Mindless creatures are immune.*

**Burning Judgment Eyes (Su)**
Always active. Functions simultaneously as: *true seeing*, *detect evil*, *sin sight*, and aura sight (perceives divine/infernal auras by tier and intensity). Cannot be suppressed or dispelled. Shared across all three forms.

**Channel Resistance +4 / +6 (Ex)**
Custom quality reflecting Ghost Rider's fundamental nature. He occupies a space between divine and infernal that is resistant to channeled energy from either direction. Functions as the undead trait but applies to a living outsider. Not derived from creature type — it is a property of his cosmological position.

**Abyssal Resurrection / Seed of Life — Rider's Vigil (Su)**
If slain, Ghost Rider eventually reconstitutes. Unlike standard Abyssal Resurrection (keyed to an Abyssal realm), his resurrection is keyed to his divine commission. So long as God's will for vengeance persists, so does Ghost Rider. Timeline of reconstitution is at GM discretion — narrative rather than mechanical.
